

1. **LeetCode**: Offers daily coding challenges and a wide range of problems that can help improve your algorithmic thinking and coding skills. They also have mock interviews and company-specific problems.

   Website: [LeetCode](https://leetcode.com/)

2. **HackerRank**: Provides coding challenges across various domains, including algorithms, data structures, artificial intelligence, and databases. They also have a section for projects where you can work on real-world problems.

   Website: [HackerRank](https://www.hackerrank.com/)

3. **Codewars**: A platform where you can solve coding challenges (called "kata") of varying difficulty. It also allows you to see other users' solutions and discuss different approaches.

   Website: [Codewars](https://www.codewars.com/)

4. **Project Euler**: Focuses on mathematical and computational problems that require more than just coding skills. It’s great for improving problem-solving and logical thinking.

   Website: [Project Euler](https://projecteuler.net/)

5. **Exercism**: Provides exercises in over 50 programming languages, including JavaScript and Java. It’s a great way to improve both your coding and problem-solving skills.

   Website: [Exercism](https://exercism.io/)

6. **DevProjects by Codementor**: Offers free, curated projects that you can work on to build your skills. The projects come with detailed guidelines and cover various aspects of web development, mobile development, and more.

   Website: [DevProjects](https://www.codementor.io/projects)

7. **FreeCodeCamp**: Offers a comprehensive curriculum that includes numerous projects to help you build and demonstrate your skills. They cover web development, data visualization, machine learning, and more.

   Website: [FreeCodeCamp](https://www.freecodecamp.org/)

8. **The Odin Project**: Provides a full-stack curriculum with project-based learning. It's great for getting hands-on experience with real-world projects.

   Website: [The Odin Project](https://www.theodinproject.com/)

These platforms will help you practice coding regularly and build projects to enhance your skills.
